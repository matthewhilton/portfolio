Acrylic Pour & EL wire Desk Pieces

Home / Acrylic Pour & El Wire

Combinations of flowing colours and night time glow

It's pretty hard to come up with new stuff, so being creative is about finding good matches between mediums.

El wire art traditionally involves a lot of messy hot glue and ends up with wobbly looking lines. Acrylic Pour art generally looks pretty good (if you choose the right colours that is), except it tends to lack the real 'glow' that other art has. Not glow in the sense that it lights up, but instead it tends to lack a real sense of story behind it; there is only so much you can make of abstract lines.


By comining the two, you can get a real sense of a story behind it, in addition to the amazing abstract lines that acrylic pours make. It also helps the EL wire to have a bit more substance than just the light.

These pieces are my experimentation and development (via trial and error), trying to find a method that would consistently produce high quality pieces
