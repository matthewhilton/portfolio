My work

My past work involves a mix of design, making, and programming; comining hardware and software

showhost

node.js based game show software

My largest project so far. Showhost was made in response to a need for better management of a school quiz gameshow. Utilises node.js and socket.io for communication. Written using simple javascript (no fancy frameworks sorry!).

lucent alleys hidden

2018 art installation

Lucent alleys hidden is an installation capturing the essence of an alleyway at night, lit only by city lights around it. Made from EL wire and acrylic tubes bent into letters using heat gun.

Three Cubes

Art Series

Three cubes is an exploration into light and shapes. The cubes comprise of 3 different ways light interacts: wire mesh, infinity mirrors and floating beams.
