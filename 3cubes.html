Three Cubes Art Project

Home / Three Cubes

Three Cubes is an exploration into light and shapes

Completed at part of my second semester art project in Grade 12, Three Cubes was designed out of the idea of 'synergy'.


Three Cubes utilises individually adressable Neopixels, Arduino microcontrollers, and various different light interacting elements (and don't forget over 1000 ping pong balls!)


Cube 1 is the 'Mesh Cube', constructed out of wire mesh, welded together and spraypainted white.

Cube 2 is the 'Mirror Cube', made from 5 pieces of clear acrylic lined with a semi reflective film, creating an infinity mirror inside the cube. It appears as though the sea of ping pong balls goes on forever...

Cube 3 is the 'Beam Cube', made from 5 pieces of acrylic joined onto a PVC tube frame, with small 'beams' of acrylic tube with white EL wire inside.


Three Cubes was a learning experience for me as I had never welded before, and learning how to apply film without creating air pockets was definetely a challenge.
